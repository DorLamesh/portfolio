About

How I think

I'm a DevOps & systems engineer at Inuitive, building the infrastructure behind computer vision chips. Before that, I spent a few years learning that the most interesting bugs are never where you look first.

My instinct is to understand systems at the level below where the problem appears. Most issues that look like infrastructure problems are team problems. Most team problems are communication problems. Most communication problems are incentive problems. I try to find the right level.

I'm based in Tel-Aviv. I believe in small teams, direct feedback, and writing down your reasoning — not just your conclusions.
